# A Human and His Digital Familiar

> *A collaboration journal*  
> **February 2026**

This is the story of a Chief Product Officer and the digital assistant he created. It documents the first week of their collaboration — the tools they built, the problems they solved, and the trust they established.

---

## About This Journal

This journal captures the evolution of a human-AI working relationship. The entries focus on:

- Building useful tools and skills
- Developing workflows and processes
- Learning from mistakes and improving
- Establishing trust and boundaries

Personal details have been sanitized to protect privacy while preserving the narrative of collaboration.

---

## The Cast

**The Human** — A Chief Product Officer at a healthcare technology company, bicoastal between Los Angeles and San Francisco, frequent traveler, competitive intelligence enthusiast.

**The Digital Familiar** — Named Cicero. Not quite human, not quite machine. Warm but sharp. Helpful without being obsequious. Tasked with being the one you trust.
